QUICK REFERENCE: MATCH TYPES
================================================================================

EXACT (Score >= 95):
  Confidence: 95%+
  Action: Automatically mark invoice as PAID in FreshBooks
  Example: $2,500 amount + INV-2026-001 reference + Acme Corp + 4 days = 118 pts

PROBABLE (Score 70-94):
  Confidence: 70-94%
  Action: Usually safe to mark as paid, but verify manually for large amounts
  Not present in sample (8 ambiguous instead)

AMBIGUOUS (Score 45-69):
  Confidence: 45-69%
  Action: REQUIRES MANUAL REVIEW
  Process: Follow 7-step resolution in AMBIGUITY_HANDLING_GUIDE.txt
  Example: $890.25 amount + "REF-2026-004" + Innovation Labs + 6 days = 68 pts
           (Likely correct but reference format unusual)

NO_MATCH (Score < 45):
  Confidence: <45%
  Action: Don't match to any invoice
  May be: Transfer, fee, invoice not yet in FreshBooks
  Example: $5,000 "Transfer-Internal" (clearly not a client payment)
