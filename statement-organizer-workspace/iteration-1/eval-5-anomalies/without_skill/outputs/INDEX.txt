================================================================================
FIVE ANOMALY DETECTION METHODS IMPLEMENTED
================================================================================

1. STATISTICAL OUTLIER DETECTION (Z-Score)
   Identifies amounts significantly outside normal spending range
   Example: $5,000 charge when average is $50
   Severity: Based on Z-score (# of standard deviations)

2. MERCHANT PATTERN ANALYSIS
   Flags new merchants and rarely-used vendors
   Example: First-ever charge to "Crypto Exchange Platform"
   Severity: 0.6 for new, 0.4 for rare merchants

3. TIME-BASED ANOMALY DETECTION
   Catches transactions at unusual hours
   Example: 3:22 AM charge when normal hours are 9am-3pm
   Severity: 0.35 based on historical frequency

4. DUPLICATE DETECTION
   Identifies identical transactions within 24 hours
   Example: Two identical $42.50 Target charges at same time
   Severity: 0.8 (high - clear error or fraud indicator)

5. FREQUENCY & CONCENTRATION ANALYSIS
   Detects unusual clustering of high-value transactions
   Example: 25% of transactions are 2x average amount
   Severity: 0.5 based on concentration ratio

================================================================================
DETECTION METHODOLOGY SUMMARY
================================================================================

Statistical Analysis:
- Z-score threshold: 2.5 standard deviations
- Includes 98.8% of legitimate transactions
- Catches unusually large/small amounts

Merchant Tracking:
- Compares against 3+ months historical baseline
- Flags vendors never seen before (frequency = 0)
- Warns on rarely-used merchants (frequency < 2)

Time-Based:
- Analyzes transaction hour distribution
- Flags hours with <20% historical frequency
- Catches 3am charges when user typically shops 9am-3pm

Duplicate Detection:
- Matches exact amount + merchant within 24 hours
- High severity (0.8) due to clear indicator
- Strong action: Contact bank to reverse

Frequency Analysis:
- Calculates concentration of high-value transactions
- Flags if >30% of transactions are 2x average
- Indicates coordinated fraud activity
