KEY TAKEAWAY
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

    for learning and remembering bank statement formats.

    Process:
    1. Read CSV headers once
    2. Use pattern matching to identify columns
    3. Save mapping to persistent storage
    4. Reuse forever for all future statements

    Result: Zero manual intervention after first statement from each bank
